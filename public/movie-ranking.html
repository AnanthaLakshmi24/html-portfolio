The Best Movies According to Anantha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sita Ramam

This is my favourite Movie.This romantic drama set against the backdrop of war tells the story of a soldier and his love for a woman named Sita. Its beautiful storytelling and performances, especially by Dulquer Salmaan and Mrunal Thakur, make it a standout film..

Leader

Most inspiring movie about the corrupted politics and how he sustains and becomes a Leader.

Janatha Garage

Super beautiful film.About Environment and problems faced by People.
